Discover Our Destinations

Mount Popa

An extinct volcano known as the spiritual home of Myanmar's nats.

Mahamuni Temple

One of the most sacred Buddhist pilgrimage sites in Myanmar.

Mandalay Moat

The royal palace surrounded by a beautiful water moat and stone walls.

Kaunghmudaw Pagoda

A massive hemispherical dome pagoda located in Sagaing.

Maha Bodhi Ta Htaung

Home to the giant standing Buddha statue in Monywa.

Pyin Oo Lwin

Pyin Oo Lwin (Maymyo) is a scenic, cool-weather town known for its flowers and historic architecture.

Kyauktawgyi Pagoda

Notable for its great marble Buddha image near the Mandalay Hill.

Bago Shwemawdaw Pagoda

The tallest pagoda in Myanmar, located in the historic city of Bago.

Sule Pagoda

A famous golden pagoda located in the heart of Yangon City.

Pho Win Taung Caves

A historic cave complex near Monywa, famous for its Buddha paintings and playful monkeys.

Kuthodaw Pagoda

Known as the world's largest book with hundreds of marble stone tablets.

Mingun Pahtodawgyi

A massive unfinished pagoda famous for its grand size near the Ayeyarwady River.
